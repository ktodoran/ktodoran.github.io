Boundaries / safety posture

Constraints are the product. They’re how AI becomes usable in production.

Default constraints

  • No checkout or payments automation
  • No medical/legal claims
  • No sensitive personal data unless explicitly designed for it
  • Scope-limited answers only (grounded sources)

Reliability measures

  • Grounding + citations (or “based on” signals)
  • Escalation when confidence is low
  • Audit-friendly logging and review loops
  • Monitoring hooks to track failures + gaps
