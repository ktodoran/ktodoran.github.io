Boundaries / safety posture

The fastest way to create risk is to let AI “do everything.” I design systems to be useful while staying inside clear constraints.

Default constraints

  • No checkout or payments automation
  • No handling of sensitive personal data unless explicitly designed for it
  • No medical/legal claims; policy-grounded responses only
  • Refuse/redirect when outside approved scope

Reliability measures

  • Source-grounded answers (citations / “what this is based on”)
  • Escalation to human support when uncertain
  • Audit-friendly logging for quality and safety review
  • Monitoring hooks to track failures and retrain retrieval
