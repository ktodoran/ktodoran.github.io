Boundaries / safety posture

Constraints are how AI becomes usable in production. Defaults are conservative; scope expands intentionally.

Default constraints

  • No checkout or payments automation
  • No medical or legal claims
  • No sensitive personal data unless explicitly designed for
  • Scope-limited, source-grounded answers only

Reliability measures

  • Grounding + citations (or “based on” signals)
  • Escalation when confidence is low
  • Audit-friendly logging for quality + safety review
  • Monitoring hooks for failure analysis and iteration

How I work

Two phases: ship a safe baseline quickly, then optimize based on real user behavior.
